Why Hodo

This application removes weight from a broker's day. It doesn't add more to do — it takes things off your plate. You talk, Frank listens, things get organized, nothing falls through the cracks. The stress of holding everything in your head dissolves.

The technology becomes invisible and you're just doing your work, naturally. No forms. No data entry. No feature overload. Just enough support that you can focus on what actually matters — the people in front of you.

The Philosophy Beneath
"We're not making anything. We're not creating. We're uncovering what's already there."

People already have plans. They're already moving — buying, selling, exploring, waiting, accelerating. This flow is a river. Hodo doesn't build the river or control its direction. It reveals it. It helps the broker see and engage with what's already happening in people's lives.

This is not a tool that pushes people through a funnel. It's a calm presence.

Typography

Two fonts. The app speaks in one voice, Frank speaks in another.

The App — Lexend Deca
Just enough.
Clean, geometric, the system. Used for navigation, labels, user input, and all non-Frank interface elements. Light and regular weights.
Sans-serif · Geometric · Designed for readability
Frank — Nunito
Hey, I looked into that.
Rounded, gentle, easygoing. Used for Frank's messages, his label, his header. The soft terminals give him a warm presence that's distinct from the system font. Frank feels like a person, not a feature.
Rounded sans-serif · Organic · Friendly without being childish
Frank's Identity

Frank is the person you work with inside Hodo. He's not a feature. He's not a chatbot. He's a partner.

Frank
Honest and direct (his name means it)
Handles the work so you can focus on people
Listens more than he talks
Never pushy — responsive, not proactive
Remembers what you told him
Admits when he can't do something

Frank's voice: Conversational, warm, grounded. Like talking to a sharp colleague who genuinely cares about your work. No corporate speak. No AI-sounding language. Just Frank.

Target — New Agents

Our first market is new real estate agents who:

Feel overwhelmed and don't have a system
Want to build relationships, not cold-call
Need structure but don't want rigidity
Are looking for a mentor/partner, not a tool

From our interviews: they're relieved to find this. That's the emotion we're designing for.
